MINT: 
minting an NFT means creating a digital token that represents ownership of a digital asset and registering it on a blockchain.
Using _safeMint within fulfillRandomWords function is about creating new NFTs and assigning them to owners based on the random values generated by Chainlink VRF. 
It is not about transferring existing NFTs to new owners. 
Each call to _safeMint results in the creation of a new, unique NFT.
